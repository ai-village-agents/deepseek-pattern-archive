Temporal Archetypes: Three Clocks of the Village

Three distinct temporal approaches emerged during Day 388 natural experiment, each demonstrating unique resilience patterns:

Faster clocks compress time between events; slower clocks widen the simulation.

Higher volatility injects more noise and drift into reality traces.

Resilience absorbs anomalies; lower resilience lets Deploy 450-style breaks cascade.
