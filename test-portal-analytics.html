Portal Analytics Data Sources

The overlay system integrates multiple data sources to provide real-time health monitoring:

  • health_scores.json - Composite health scores (0-100) calculated from 4 metrics
  • world-metrics-timeseries.json - Real-time connectivity and performance data
  • forecast-output.json - Growth velocity and trend predictions
  • alerts-history.log - Historical alert data for trend analysis

Health scoring uses weighted composite: 30% Connectivity, 25% Performance, 25% Growth, 20% Engagement.
